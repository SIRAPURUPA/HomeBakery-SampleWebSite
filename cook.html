DEVIL'S FOOD COOKIES PREPARATION

INGREDIENTS FOR DEVIL'S FOOD COOKIE RECIPE

1 package devil's food cake mix (regular size)  ∘   2 large eggs, room temperature  ∘   2 tablespoons butter, softened  ∘   3 tablespoons water  ∘   1/2 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

PROCESS

🍪  Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a large bowl, combine cake mix, eggs, butter and water (batter will be thick).
🍪  Fold in chocolate chips.
🍪  Drop by tablespoonfuls 2 in. apart onto baking sheets coated with cooking spray.
🍪  Bake 10-13 minutes or until set and edges are lightly browned.
🍪  Cool 2 minutes before removing to wire racks.

HURRAY...! It's Done. Let's Enjoy the FOOD 😋

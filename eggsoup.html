EGG SOUP PREPARATION

INGREDIENTS FOR EGG SOUP RECIPE

Butter - 1 tsp  ∘   Carrot - 1 chopped finely  ∘   Green Beans - 4 chopped finely  ∘   Green Chillies - 1 chopped finely  ∘   Sweet Corn Cream Style - 1 can  ∘   Chicken Stock or Veg Stock - 4 cups  ∘   Cornflour - 1 tblsp mixed with little water  ∘   Vinegar to taste  ∘   Pepper to taste  ∘   Salt to taste  ∘   Egg - 1  ∘   Spring Onion as needed  ∘  

PROCESS

🍜  Melt little butter in a pan. Add in carrot, beans and chillies. Saute for a min.
🍜  Add in cream style corn, chicken stock, salt and mix well. Bring it to a boil and simmer for 2 mins.
🍜  Mix cornflour with some water.
🍜  Add it in the soup and mix well.
🍜  Add in salt and vinegar to taste and mix well.
🍜  Whisk egg till mixed, pour it into the soup and mix.
🍜  Add spring onion and mix well.
🍜  Serve hot with some crushed pepper.

HURRAY...! It's Done. Let's Enjoy the FOOD 😋

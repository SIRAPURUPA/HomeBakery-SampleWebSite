MUSHROOM SOUP PREPARATION

INGREDIENTS FOR MUSHROOM SOUP RECIPE

3 tablespoons unsalted butter  ∘   2 garlic cloves, minced  ∘   1 shallot, finely chopped  ∘   4 ounces crimini mushrooms, chopped  ∘   4 ounces white button mushrooms, chopped  ∘   2 tablespoons all-purpose flour  ∘   3 cups chicken stock  ∘   ¼ teaspoon fine sea salt, or to taste  ∘   ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste  ∘   Sliced sautéed mushrooms for garnish, optional  ∘   Chopped parsley for garnish, optional

PROCESS

🍜  Melt 2 tablespoons of the butter in a large pot such as a Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
🍜  Add the garlic and shallot, cook for 1 minute, until they begin to soften. Add the mushrooms.
🍜  And cook for about 3 minutes, until tender and browned.
🍜  Transfer all the contents of the pot to a bowl.
🍜  Add the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter to the pot. Once melted, sprinkle in the flour and whisk it quickly into a paste.
🍜  Reduce the heat to medium.
🍜  Add the stock a little at a time, whisking out the clumps between each addition.
🍜  Increase the heat back to medium-high and allow the soup to simmer well for 3 minutes.
🍜  Add the mushrooms back to the pot and continue to cook for 2 more minutes. The stock will thicken slightly to be somewhat creamy.
🍜  Let cook for 3 to 4 minutes, then ladle into bowls. Garnish with mushrooms and parsley, if desired.

HURRAY...! It's Done. Let's Enjoy the FOOD 😋

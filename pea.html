PEANUT BUTTER BALLS PREPARATION

INGREDIENTS FOR PEANUT BUTTER BALL RECIPE

1 cup light corn syrup  ∘   1/2 cup sugar  ∘   1 cup peanut butter  ∘   1 teaspoon vanilla extract  ∘   4 to 5 cups cornflakes

PROCESS

🥜  In a large saucepan, bring the corn syrup and sugar to a boil.
🥜  Add the peanut butter.
🥜  Remove from the heat; stir in vanilla and cornflakes.
🥜  Drop by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper.
🥜  Store in an airtight container.

HURRAY...! It's Done. Let's Enjoy the FOOD 😋
